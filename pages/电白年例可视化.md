#### 走公Zou Gong
在当地“走”有跑的意思，年例中的走公主要指在年例期间，村民们会抬着神像进行竞跑，以祈求神灵的庇，场面热闹激烈，所到之处鞭炮声四起。
In the local dialect, "zou" means "to run." During the Nianli, the activity of "zou gong" mainly refers to villagers carrying the deity statues and engaging in a race. This is done to seek divine protection. The scene is lively and intense, with firecrackers going off everywhere they go.
